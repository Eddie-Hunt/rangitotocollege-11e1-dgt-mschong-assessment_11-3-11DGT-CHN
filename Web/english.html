About English

NCEA Level 1 English helps students develop their reading, writing, and communication skills. You’ll study novels, poems, and films while learning how language creates meaning. You’ll also practise writing for different audiences and purposes, preparing you for future study and everyday communication.

Important Quotes & Techniques

Simile: “Her eyes shone like stars.” – compares two things to create imagery.

Metaphor: “Time is a thief.” – gives deeper meaning through comparison.

Personification: “The wind whispered through the trees.” – gives human qualities to objects or nature.

Imagery: “The golden sunlight spilled across the field.” – helps the reader picture the scene.

Essay Writing Tips

  • Use the PEEL Point, Evedence, Explain, Link.
  • Start paragraphs with a clear topic sentence.
  • Use linking words like “This shows…”, “This suggests…”, “Therefore…”.
  • Focus on how and why the author uses language techniques.
