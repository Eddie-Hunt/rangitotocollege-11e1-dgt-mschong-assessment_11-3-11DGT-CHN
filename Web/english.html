About English

NCEA Level 1 English is a New Zealand secondary school qualification focused on developing students' reading, writing, and communication skills. It involves studying and analyzing various texts like novels and films, as well as creating original written and visual texts for different purposes and audiences. The course aims to build understanding of how language works and how to use it effectively in different contexts, preparing students for future study and work
