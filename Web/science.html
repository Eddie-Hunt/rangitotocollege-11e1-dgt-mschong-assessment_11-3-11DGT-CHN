About Science

NCEA Level 1 Science is a New Zealand qualification that provides an introduction to foundational concepts across the sciences, including physics, chemistry, biology, and earth and space science. Students learn to investigate scientific phenomena, conduct experiments, and develop a broad understanding of how science operates and its application in the world. This is equivalent to Year 11 of secondary school.
