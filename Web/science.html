Science

Explore the wonders of the natural world

About Science

NCEA Level 1 Science helps students understand how the world works through physics, chemistry, and biology. You’ll explore how matter behaves, how energy moves, and how living things interact with their environments. The focus is on using scientific methods to ask questions, test ideas, and draw conclusions that connect to real life.

Key Topics

Physics

Learn about motion, forces, and energy. Physics explains how objects move and interact — from cars on the road to planets in space.

Chemistry

Understand atoms, elements, and chemical reactions. Chemistry helps you see how substances combine and change to form new materials.

Biology

Study living things, their cells, and how they survive and adapt. Biology shows the connection between humans, animals, and the environment.
